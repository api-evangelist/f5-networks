DNS weaknesses remain critical infrastructure vulnerabilities, particularly when combined with AI systems. The author argues that name resolution failures cascade through entire systems, and AI magnifies these risks due to rapid infrastructure changes, global distribution requirements, security implications, scaling complexity, and cascading dependencies.
