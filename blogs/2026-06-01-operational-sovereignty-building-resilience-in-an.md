Organizations are shifting beyond traditional data sovereignty to embrace operational sovereignty - the ability to maintain business continuity when conditions change unexpectedly due to regulatory shifts, cloud-provider restrictions, infrastructure outages, and evolving AI governance. F5's Application Delivery and Security Platform (ADSP) enables portability and consistency so applications and policies can move across hybrid multicloud environments while maintaining unified security enforcement and traffic management.
