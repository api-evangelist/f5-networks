As AI drives unprecedented data volumes into S3-compatible storage, traffic demands now exceed what storage systems alone can manage. F5 is enhancing BIG-IP with native S3 commands and events in iRules coming later this year, and publishing deployment guides for Dell ObjectScale and MinIO, positioning the load balancer as infrastructure between compute and storage.
